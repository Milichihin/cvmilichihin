who am I

Hi there!

Firstly, I’m very glad you are here. I’ll tell you a little bit about myself.

Here you’ll see my skills in front-end. Of course, I couldn't show all my knowledge in one place, and I don’t planned. It was build by pure HTML, CSS and JS. There are no any library, frameworks and such instruments in spite of its usefulness.

And more couple of words about the site. An animation for time-line slide in a section “experience”,as well as curtain menu and a slice scrolling on mobile (I hope you have already seen and appreciated it, haha), automatic slide for active menu item – all this things was written through JQuery. However, a burger button (look at top left corner if you use a smartphone now) is pure CSS.

Before I've seriously stepped into the WEB, for a long time I’m looking around this area. Early I permanently faced the necessary to support a media project in WEB, either landing pages or small product sites, and of course ad formats and their adaptation for different internet platforms.

By the will of destiny, I moved from Kyiv to Lviv, and took a brake from my main job. At that time, I got interested in layout and design that inevitably led to the thoughts about relevant courses. I was lucky, I got a course “Web Development” at the SoftServe IT Academy. At course I saw a bit of real world of front-end with JS, JQuery, AJAX and others technologies. And since then WEB already not seems just like usual source of media content. The more I learn, the more I understand that learning and self-develop is continuous never-ending process. At this point I remember the Dunning–Kruger Effect.

Whatever I’m always glad to meet a new people, therefore my contact information is at the bottom of this page.

We'll be in touch.
